About Our School

Empowering generations through education since 1931

Our Legacy

Government Higher Secondary School, Chitlapakkam was established in 1931 and has stood as a pillar of public education in Tamil Nadu for over 90 years. Administered by the Education Department, our mission is to provide knowledge, values, and confidence to students from rural and suburban backgrounds, building a brighter future.

We follow the Tamil language and state board curriculum, serving classes 6 through 10 with 14 dedicated teachers. While rooted in tradition, we continuously evolve to remain relevant to modern educational needs.

Our school serves as an integral part of the Chitlapakkam community, providing equal opportunities to every child. Our alumni have excelled in various fields, standing testament to the success of our educational approach.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education to all regardless of background, and to develop confident, curious, and compassionate learners. Our goal is to create an environment where every child can achieve their full potential.

Our Vision

To become a model government school that integrates values, education, technology, and social harmony. We dream of our students being educated to honor Tamil heritage while facing the challenges of the modern world.
