How We Teach

Our teachers follow the Tamil Nadu State Board curriculum with structured lesson planning. We believe in activity-based learning, blackboard interactions, and regular evaluations to ensure progress.

Emphasis is placed on values, discipline, and inclusive teaching for slow learners and advanced students alike. Our 14 teachers (4 male, 10 female) are trained in modern pedagogical methods while respecting traditional teaching values.
